1. FACULTY

The school has a team of teachers drawn from the best talent pool of the country. They are highly talented, qualified, competent and committed to give the best to every student. Personal care, attention and concern for the complete development of every child are the hall mark of the education that the school provides.

2. SCHOOL RULES AND REGULATIONS:

School Timings:

The School academic year is divided into two terms i.e. Term I and Term II. Term I begins on June 01, 2013 and closes on October 2013. The term II begins on October 30, 2013 and closes on March 28, 2014. The school activities and holidays are mentioned in the school calendar for reference. The school timings are from 08:30AM to 04:00PM from Monday to Friday and from 08:30AM to 12:15PM on Saturday. The school timings must be strictly adhered to by all.

Uniform:

All students must be neatly dressed in full school uniform whenever they are in school premises. Students are required to wear regular uniform with black shoes and black socks from Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday and school sports dress along with white canvas shoes and white socks on Wednesday and Saturday. Colour clothes and fancy, expensive footwear will not be allowed. Fancy or expensive watches, colourful jerkins or different shades of contact lenses and trolley bags will not be permitted.

Girls are not permitted to:

Have streaked hair, fancy hair style, leaves shoulder length or long hair untied; Wear eye makeup and more than one ear piercing; Wear gold earrings, bangles and chains, colourful and fancy jewellery, long earrings; Adorn their hair with flower or colourful clips, painted nails or mehendi.

Boys are not permitted to:

Have long side burns, long hair, streaked hair and fancy hair style; Wear bands/ bracelets/ chains; Grow their beard, moustache.
